# Quick Start Guide - Global Market Expansion Platform

## 5-Minute Quick Start

### Step 1: Open the Platform
- Open your web browser and navigate to the platform URL
- Wait for the dashboard to load (about 5-10 seconds)

### Step 2: Explore the Main Dashboard
- You'll see 32 global markets organized by regions
- Each market shows: Index value, Change, Change %, Volume, Status
- Green numbers indicate positive changes, red indicates negative

### Step 3: Try Basic Features

#### View Different Regions
- Click on region tabs: Asia, Europe, Americas, Africa, Middle East, Oceania
- See how different markets are performing in each region

#### Check Market Status
- Look at the "Status" column: Open (green), Closed (gray), Holiday (orange)
- See which markets are currently active for trading

#### Analyze a Specific Market
- Click on any market (e.g., NYSE, London, Tokyo)
- View detailed information about that market

### Step 4: Use Advanced Features

#### Trading Hours Optimization
- Navigate to the "Trading Hours Optimization" tab
- See when markets overlap for maximum trading opportunities
- Check liquidity levels during different time periods

#### AI Analysis
- Go to the "Enhanced AI Integration" tab
- Select a market from the dropdown
- Click "Analyze Market" to get AI-powered predictions
- Review the risk analysis and trading opportunities

#### Performance Monitoring
- Visit the "Performance Optimization" tab
- Monitor system health and performance metrics
- Run tests to ensure system stability

## Key Terms Explained

### Market Terms
- **Index**: A benchmark representing market performance
- **Volume**: Number of shares/contracts traded
- **Market Cap**: Total market value of all companies
- **Change**: Difference from previous closing price
- **Change %**: Percentage change from previous close

### Status Indicators
- **Open**: Market is currently active for trading
- **Closed**: Market is not currently trading
- **Holiday**: Market is closed for holiday

### AI Terms
- **Confidence**: How certain the AI prediction is (0-100%)
- **Risk Level**: Low, Medium, or High risk assessment
- **Prediction**: AI forecast of market direction
- **Signal**: AI recommendation for trading action
