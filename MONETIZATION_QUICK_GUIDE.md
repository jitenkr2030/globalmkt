## üé® Pricing Psychology Tips

### Anchor Pricing
- Show Enterprise plan ($2,999) to make Professional ($499) seem reasonable
- Display "most popular" badge on Professional plan
- Show annual savings (15-25% discount) to encourage longer commitments

### Value Stacking
- Bundle features to show higher value
- Use tiered pricing to encourage upgrades
- Show ROI calculations (e.g., "Save 20 hours/month = $2,000 value")

### Urgency and Scarcity
- Limited-time offers for new customers
- Feature availability based on plan level
- Early bird pricing for annual commitments
